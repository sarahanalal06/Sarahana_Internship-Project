# Sarahana_Internship-Project
Project Title: Stock Market & Cryptocurrency
By: Sarahana Lal

This presentation will enable a comprehensive study of stock market trading and cryptocurrency investments by analyzing different technical aspects in relation to cryptocurrency investments. The presentation will create an opportunity to learn how to make decisions in stock market trading.
Key Topics Covered:
Basic Concepts of Stock Market
explanation of stocks and shares
How investors acquire and transfer ownership in firms
Technical Indicators
Magic Band Indicator:
Makes predictions about the trend, levels of support and resistance, and possible breaches or reversals
Relative Strength Index (RSI)
Measures momentum & overbought & oversold levels
Used to set target levels of profit and exit points
Trading Strategy Examples
__________________
Reliance Industries Ltd.: Entry, exit, and stop-loss strategy using Magic Band, RSI, and Fibonacci levels
Hindustan Copper: Similar strategy for risk control and trend confirmation
Strategy Summary
----------------------------
Trend following (Magic Band), momentum confirmation (RSI), and positioning of targets (Fibonacci/Price Resistance)
Emphasis on disciplined risk management and favorable risk-reward ratios
Risk Management
Risk
Importance of Identifying, Analyzing, and Controlling Risks
Methods for lessening losses & enhancing decision-making
Conclusion:
Technical analysis and risk management instruments enable the making of well-informed decisions
Well-structured strategies with correct entries, targets, and stop losses enhance trading results in stock markets and cryptocurrencies
